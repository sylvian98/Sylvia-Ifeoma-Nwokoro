Weetabix

Learn More Page

While working at a design and brand agency called Honey, I was responsible for maintaining and updating the Weetabix websites. Honey manages a total of 21 sites, with Weetabix.com serving as the main international site where users can select their preferred language.

The client requested a Learn More page featuring a pop-up linking to three different pages on the international site. Using ACF, PHP, HTML, SCSS, and JavaScript, I built a custom page template that allows users to select their spoken language from a dropdown and choose which page to navigate to. The pop-up also includes a close button, ensuring a smooth user experience if the wrong language is selected.
